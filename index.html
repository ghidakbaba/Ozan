About him

Biography

Early life & education

Ozan Akbaba was born in Kars, Turkey, on June 9, 1982. He graduated from Akdeniz University’s Faculty of Fine Arts, Department of Interior Architecture and Environmental Design.

Acting identity

He is best known to many viewers for portraying İlyas Çakırbeyli in Eşkıya Dünyaya Hükümdar Olmaz, a role that became one of the major milestones of his TV career.

Beyond acting

Alongside acting, Akbaba has worked in music and composition, with credits connected to film and television projects.
